About Us

Our Story

At Rolasan Resto, we are dedicated to bringing the rich, aromatic, and diverse flavors of Indonesia to your plate. Our story begins with a passion for authentic Indonesian cuisine and a commitment to sharing it with the world. With deep-rooted family recipes and a dedication to quality, we have become a beloved gem in the culinary landscape.

Our Ambiance

Immerse yourself in the warm and welcoming atmosphere of Indonesia right here in Magelang. Our restaurant is designed to transport you to the beautiful archipelago with its rustic decor, traditional artwork, and soothing music. Every visit is an experience.
